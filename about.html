Our Members

Kevin mcguire - vocals/guitar

The founder of the band and the vocalist. He is 31 and from the Dumbarton area. He has sung for many bands before, however he has found the most success with Century Thirteen. He has many aspirations for the band and hopes to go on international tours with the band someday. His infulences include: The Offspring, Sum 41 and Bowling For Soup.

scott mcleod - guitar/vocals

Scott is 32 and from the Coatbridge area. He enjoys playing guitar as much onstage as he does off stage, he is working on some solo projects while the band has downtime. His influences are: Bring Me The Horizon, Metallica and Arch Enemy.

dale gardner - bass

Dale 33 is from the Maryhill area. He is a huge fan of Pokemon and is known for his trademark hat. He also serves as the main booker for the band. Influences include: AC/DC and Audioslave.

Lewis Harwood - Drums

Lewis is 26 and from the Kirkintilloch area. His bandmates describe him as the energy of the group. The also affectionately refer to him as the "dafty" of the group due to his free nature. His influences include: Machine head and Stone Sour.
