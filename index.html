Hi everyone. We have been quiet since September when we played the garage. This year has been the toughest year we have had as a band with the stress of bring out a album ,each of us having personal issues, there was always people who supported us, took pictures, shared our statues and showed up to watch us play we want to say thank you we appreciate it very much. As for the future we have decided to take a break from doing Century Thirteen as we have been non stop for 4 years we have added a picture album of the best things we have experienced in this band. We will return but til then take care everyone.

Big Shout out to everyone who came out last nite to watch us and to the rest of the bands for making this a cool nite twister are outstanding and Ordinary hero were incredible as well but Phosphenes were the true heros of the nite for us letting us share there equitment, letting me borrow a guitar when my string snapped but most importantly playing the ramones.
