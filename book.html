Booking us

how to contact

Thinking about book a band? Look no further! We have experience in playing at weddings and partys! You can contact us via Facebook or email

waiting time

Typically we like to reply within 3-5 days. Can be longer if our schedule is busy at weekends. However if we can reply to you sooner we will!

what happens next?

Once we recieve a request for a booking we check our schedule, if we are available for your chosen dates we will get back to you and begin dicussing logistics and pricing.

About
